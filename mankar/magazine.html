College Magazine

The College Magazine committee works to bring out the annual official student-publication "Uttaran" of Mankar College. Each year, our team of editors, designers, photographers, and correspondents, in addition to generating creative content from the student population, work extensively to report on events in and around college. The final publication reflects and encompasses the diversity inherent to the academic and extra-curricular spaces. The magazine "Uttaran" continues to expand its reach to achieve its vision of being a truly representative of student publication. The Magazine committee hopes to build on this ethos just as much during the upcoming academic years.
